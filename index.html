About

Fake Café is a new and upcoming Café based in The Netherlands with currently a single location in Maastricht. The reason for only having a single location is that the café is not actually a real café but a imaginary café. It only exists as an idea that was made up for a English project at school. The reason for making a website about a café is that I always enjoyed the calming and relaxing idea of cafes and to be honest could not think of anything else right away. But hopefully even though it is not a real café you will still enjoy our website!.

History

Since Fake Café is not an actually café you can visit but just an idea for a project I made up there’s not much history to our café. Although by now the café would be around a month old and still going strong with hopefully lots and lots of customers buying and enjoying all our products on the menu! We also believe something doesn’t need to have a strong history to be competitive these days in the market as long as you can satisfy the customer needs en put a smile on their face when they leave.
